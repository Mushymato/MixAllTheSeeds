## [0.4.0] - 2026-05-06

### Added
- New config Mix_ExcludeRegrowing, prevent mixed seeds from planting regrowing crops.
- New config Mix_ExcludeRare, prevent mixed seeds from planting seeds you can't buy from Pierre's/Joja.
